Script Smart uses advanced algorithms to automatically format your text into a screenplay format. With Script Smart, you can save time and focus on writing by letting the software handle the formatting for you. The Automatic Screenplay Breakdown (Break Smart) is a time-saving solution that simplifies the process of deconstructing a screenplay. It automatically dissects your script into key elements like scenes, characters, and locations, providing a clear and organized overview of your story's structure.
